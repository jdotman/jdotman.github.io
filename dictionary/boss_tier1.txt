The ancient dragon stirs from slumber, shaking the earth beneath your feet. Steel your resolve, brave warrior, for this battle will test your spirit.

The dark fortress shudders as its master awakens. Every stone, every shadow here bends to his will. Face your fear, warrior, or be consumed by it.
