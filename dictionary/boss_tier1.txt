# Boss paragraphs — Tier 1 (Level 5)
The ancient dragon lifts its great head, shaking the cavern walls with a deafening roar.
Beware, lone hero — the goblin king has rallied his dark forces, and shadow spreads fast across the land. Only you can stop him now.
A great beast blocks the path ahead, its deep growl shaking loose dust from the cavern ceiling above. Stand tall, champion, for you carry the hopes of the entire besieged village on your shoulders today.
The dark fortress shudders as its master awakens deep within its stone heart. Every shadow here bends to his will, every torch dims at his approach. They say no warrior has ever left these halls alive, and they may be right. Steel your resolve, brave fighter, for tonight you will prove them wrong.
The creature that guards this final gate — the one whose name was struck from every scroll long before your ancestors arrived, whose shadow has darkened this valley for longer than memory reaches — turns its ancient eyes toward you; and whatever courage you carried to this threshold, you will need every last fragment of it before this ends.
