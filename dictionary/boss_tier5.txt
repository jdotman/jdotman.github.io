# Boss paragraphs — Tier 5 (Level 25)
Before time had a name, before the first star ignited, I already existed here.
I watched your world form from screaming dust. I taught your ancestors the meaning of fear. I am the reason nightmares exist. You have come to end me?
I am not merely powerful — power is a word mortals coined for things they cannot hold. I am the silent axiom beneath every force in this universe. Heroes far greater than you have come here as legends and left as dust.
Do you feel the cold behind your ribs? That is not fear of me — it is your body's oldest memory, a recollection of what existed before gods had names or darkness had a shape. I am that memory made real. Every instinct you possess is screaming at you to run, and every one of those instincts is correct.
Those who reach me — hardened by every battle before this one, certain that their endurance has made them unbreakable — are right about that assessment, precisely up until the moment I reveal what exists beyond the edge of what any mortal can endure; and whether you, having come this impossibly far, will flinch at that truth or keep your fingers moving is the only question that has ever mattered here.
