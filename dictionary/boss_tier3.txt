# Boss paragraphs — Tier 3 (Level 15)
From the void between worlds, the Eternal Lich surveys you with hollow eyes. 'So many heroes have stood where you now stand,' it whispers, 'and each one became part of my collection.' You grip your weapon tighter and step forward anyway.
The floor trembles as the elder dragon descends. Centuries of accumulated power radiate from its form, scorching the air. 'You've come far, little one,' it growls, 'farther than most. But this is where your story ends, and mine continues forever!'
I have watched kingdoms rise and crumble to dust. I have seen stars born and die in the void. You bring your mortal courage into my domain, and I respect that. Truly, I do. But respect and mercy are different things, and I stopped showing mercy long ago.
The god-beast turns its ancient gaze upon you, and in that moment you feel the weight of eternity. 'What is a hero?' it rumbles. 'A mayfly who dares to challenge the mountain. And yet, here you are.' It smiles. 'I haven't been surprised in centuries. Fight well.'
