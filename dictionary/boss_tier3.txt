# Boss paragraphs — Tier 3 (Level 15)
The Eternal Lich surveys you with hollow eyes, its silence heavier than any spoken threat.
So many heroes have stood exactly where you now stand, and each one, in the end, became part of my collection. Step forward.
Centuries of power radiate from the elder dragon's form, scorching the very air around it. You have come far, little one — farther than most. But this is where your story ends, and mine continues forever.
I have watched kingdoms rise and crumble to dust across ten thousand years. I have seen stars born and burn out in the void. You bring your mortal courage into my domain, and I respect it — truly. But respect and mercy are different things, and I stopped showing mercy to heroes a very long time ago.
What you perceive as power — this ancient presence that bends the air, dims the torches, and silences every bird in the forests within a day's ride — is, in truth, only the outermost edge of what I have become during the long centuries you mortals spent forgetting that I existed at all.
