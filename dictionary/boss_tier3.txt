The crystal guardian shatters into a thousand glowing shards, then reforms before your eyes and raises its blade once more.
You descend the spiral staircase into the crypt below, where the ancient vampire lord has been waiting patiently for centuries.
The war drums echo through the mountain pass as the orc warlord rides forward on a great beast of black iron and flame.
A circle of dark runes flares to life beneath your feet, and the summoned beast claws its way up from the howling void.
The ice drake breathes a wall of frost across the bridge, sealing off your only safe retreat from this deadly arena.
You shatter the old seal on the forbidden door and the darkness beyond it stirs with a low and very terrible growl.
The golem king raises both fists and slams them into the stone floor, sending deep cracks racing toward your boots.
From the shadows of the ancient library, the cursed scholar rises and hurls a crackling bolt of violet lightning at you.
The demon hound leaps from the burning wreckage and snaps its terrible jaws shut just inches from your outstretched hand.
A towering knight clad in black plate armor steps from the mist and points a gauntleted finger directly at your heart.
The sea witch summons a wall of churning dark water that crashes through the cavern and sweeps hard toward your feet.
You hear the grinding of massive bronze gears as the clockwork titan powers up and turns its glowing red eyes on you.
The shadow drake coils around the cracked stone pillar and watches you with ancient, patient, and very hungry golden eyes.
A pillar of green flame erupts from the stone altar and the lich steps through it, skull wreathed in cold pale fire.
The earth elemental tears itself free from the canyon wall and lumbers toward you with grinding, slow, and heavy steps.
