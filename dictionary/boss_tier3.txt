The obsidian wyrm coils its massive body around the crumbling pillars of the ancient hall. You raise your shield as its molten breath scorches the cold air all around you.
A spectral warden steps from the fog and swings a ghostly halberd at your armored chest. You parry the strike but the impact sends a numbing cold rippling through your arms.
The vampire lord bares his fangs and lunges across the marble floor with inhuman speed. Your blade catches him and silver light erupts from the deep wound in his side.
Cursed vines erupt from cracks in the floor and coil around your legs with crushing force. You slash them away before the dryad hurls a volley of poison thorns toward you.
The iron colossus raises its hammer and slams it down on the stone where you stood. Sparks fly from the shattered floor as you roll aside and draw your blade once more.
A wave of dark energy pulses from the necromancer and rattles every bone in the crypt. You charge through the blast and drive your sword toward the sigil on his chest.
The basilisk king turns its petrifying gaze upon you and the edges of your vision begin to harden. You avert your eyes at the last moment and strike blindly at its scaled throat with fury.
Molten chains lash out from the forge demon and wrap themselves tightly around your weapon arm. You wrench free with a shout and hurl a flask of blessed water into its burning face.
The lich queen raises her skeletal hand and the temperature in the chamber plummets below freezing. Frost races across the stone floor toward you as she begins to chant words of ancient ruin.
A chimera crashes through the stained glass window and lands among the broken pews roaring. Its lion head snaps at you while the serpent tail strikes from behind at once.
The death knight charges on a steed of black smoke swinging a cursed blade of violet flame. You brace your shield against the blow and feel dark metal screaming on your guard.
Swarms of venomous bats descend from the ceiling in a shrieking black cloud of fury. You swing your torch in wide arcs to keep them back while the queen watches.
The golem tears a boulder from the wall and hurls it across the flooded chamber at you. You dive beneath the dark water as stone fragments rain down all around you.
A towering shade emerges from the cursed mirror and raises twin blades of pure shadow. Its form flickers like a dying candle as it glides across the cracked marble floor.
The wyvern shrieks and dives from the ruined tower with talons outstretched and wings spread wide. You loose an arrow that catches her wing membrane and sends her spiraling down.
Ethereal chains burst from the ground and bind your arms as the wraith lord floats ever closer. You shatter them with a burst of willpower and swing your consecrated blade in a shining arc.
The drake slams its armored tail into the bridge and cracks race beneath your feet. You leap to the far side as the center section crumbles into the roaring chasm below.
A corrupted paladin blocks your path and slams his cracked shield against the ground with force. Dark runes flare across his armor as he raises a heavy blade bleeding thick black smoke.
The spider queen descends from the darkness on a thread of silk and hisses at you. You slash the web anchoring her to the ceiling and she crashes to the stone floor.
Bone spears erupt from the ground in a ring and begin closing inward with grinding force. You smash through the weakest point and charge toward the skeletal mage beyond them.
The hydra rears back three heads and each one gathers a different elemental fury inside. You dart between blasts of fire ice and lightning and drive your blade into its belly.
A plague demon drags itself from a festering pit and exhales green miasma across the floor. You hold your breath and rush forward to strike before the toxic fog reaches you.
The fallen seraph beats its tattered wings and sends corrupted light howling through the ruined nave. You anchor yourself behind a toppled column as loose stones tear free from the crumbling walls.
Twin gargoyles drop from the buttresses and land on either side of you claws scraping stone. You spin between them and your blade bites into one before the other can strike.
The lich raises skeletal warriors from the ossuary floor with a single sweeping gesture of command. You cut through the first wave but more drag themselves upright from the ancient dust.
A kraken tentacle smashes through the hull and gropes blindly through the flooded dark chamber. You hack at its rubbery flesh until it recoils through the breach with a shuddering groan.
The troll warlord catches your sword in its massive scarred fist and grins with broken tusks. You release the hilt and drive a burning brand into the wound before it heals.
Roots thick as pillars tear through the flagstones as the ancient treant awakens from slumber. Its bark face twists into rage and a branch the size of a mast swings at you.
The storm elemental gathers crackling bolts of lightning between its translucent hands high above the field. You sprint across the flooded courtyard knowing that the water beneath you will carry the current.
Dark tendrils snake from the portal and seize the fallen knight dragging him into the void. You sever the nearest tendril but three more surge forward to take its place at once.
You stand in a vast hall where every surface is carved with scenes of a forgotten war. The ceiling stretches so high that torchlight cannot reach it and only darkness waits above.
Pale fungi cling to the walls and pulse with a faint bioluminescent glow of sickly green. The air is thick and damp and each breath tastes of decay and something worse beneath.
The staircase descends in a tight spiral around a column of rough stone into deep earth. Faint scratching sounds echo from the darkness at the bottom like claws on bare rock.
An underground river cuts through this cavern and its black water moves with a slow current. The remains of a stone bridge jut from either bank but the center span collapsed long ago.
You enter a chamber where hundreds of candles burn in iron sconces along the walls in silence. Thick wax has pooled on the floor in ridges and the air smells of tallow and incense.
The corridor opens into a natural grotto where stalactites hang like the teeth of some vast beast. Water drips from their tips into shallow pools that reflect the faint blue light from above.
You find yourself in a library where the shelves collapsed and rotting books carpet the floor. The ink has bled from their pages and dark stains spread across the flagstones like blood.
A broken aqueduct runs along the ceiling and water streams down the walls in thin cold sheets. The stonework is green with moss and the floor has become a stream flowing into the dark.
You stand in a forge hall where the anvils are cracked and the bellows have rotted to dust. The furnace still radiates dull heat as though something deep within the coals refuses to die.
The passage narrows until you must turn sideways to squeeze between rough walls pressing your armor. Ahead the gap widens into a chamber but something large is breathing in the darkness.
You emerge onto a ledge overlooking a vast sinkhole that descends into absolute blackness far below. The wind rising from its depths carries a foul stench and the sound of something massive shifting.
A great stone door stands at the end of this hallway covered in runes that glow faintly. The hinges are rusted shut and gouges in the floor suggest someone tried to force it.
The room is a flooded crypt where stone coffins rise from dark water like islands in a lake. Their lids have been pushed aside and whatever rested within them is no longer there.
You descend into a mine shaft with rotting timber beams that creak under the weight of earth. Veins of glowing ore thread through the walls and cast shifting purple light across your path.
The chamber floor is covered in fine gray ash that rises in clouds with every step you take. It piles against the walls and wooden remains poke through the surface like bones from a grave.
A frozen garden stretches before you with ice encasing every flower and tree in crystalline stillness. The air is so cold that your breath hangs before your face like a curtain of fog.
You stand in a cistern where the water drained away long ago leaving only stained stone behind. Rusted iron grates cover drainage tunnels in the walls and faint air flows through them.
The hallway ends at a balcony overlooking a cavern so vast its far wall is lost in haze. Below a ruined city spreads across the floor with empty towers reaching up like skeletal fingers.
You step into a chamber where the walls are covered in thousands of scratched tally marks throughout. A set of manacles hangs from the far wall and the chain has worn thin from pulling.
The ruins of a bridge span a gorge filled with storm clouds that crackle with violet lightning. Only the first few spans remain and the stones are slick with rain and treacherous moss.
A vast ossuary stretches before you with bones stacked floor to ceiling in neat rows of stone. The skulls face inward and in the torchlight their empty sockets seem to follow your movement.
You enter a throne room where cobwebs drape from the chandelier to the floor like gray curtains. The throne is carved from a single block of obsidian and claw marks score its armrests.
The passage opens into a canyon beneath the earth where pale blind creatures cling to the walls. They scatter at the light of your torch and their chittering echoes in every direction.
You stand in a ritual chamber where standing stones surround a pit of absolute perfect darkness. The stones hum with a vibration you feel in your teeth and the air tastes of copper.
A collapsed watchtower blocks the passage leaving only a narrow gap choked with broken rubble ahead. Beyond it you see flickering orange light and hear a fire that no one should tend.
The floor of this chamber is a slab of polished black marble veined with threads of red crystal. Your reflection stares back but it seems to move a half second behind your steps.
You wade through a flooded tunnel where the water reaches your waist and something brushes your leg. The ceiling is low enough to touch and water stains mark a tide line above your head.
The cavern ahead glitters with raw crystal jutting from every surface in jagged pale white clusters. They chime when struck and the sound resonates through the stone like distant fragile bells.
Ancient carvings line the walls of this passage depicting warriors kneeling before a throne of black iron. The carved faces have all been chiseled away leaving only smooth blank stone where they were.
