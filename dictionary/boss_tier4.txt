# Boss paragraphs — Tier 4 (Level 20)
Since the first dawn broke across this world, I have endured and grown stronger.
Every word you have typed in haste has been a gift to me, another link in the long chain of your inevitable defeat. Continue.
They called me the Undying, the Eternal Dread, the Hunger Without End. Ten thousand warriors have tried to end my existence, and I remember each of their faces with a certain fondness. Yet here you stand.
You want to know what true power feels like? It feels like patience — absolute, unending patience. I have waited a thousand years for a worthy challenger, and none have come close. Your skill impresses me; I do not say that lightly. But impressing me and defeating me are two entirely different things.
The darkness you have fought through on every level leading here was not your enemy but your preparation — a sequence of trials I arranged, centuries in advance, to determine whether whoever finally stood before me would be someone whose defeat I would find, at minimum, interesting; you have, against all expectation, qualified.
