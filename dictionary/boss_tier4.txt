# Boss paragraphs — Tier 4 (Level 20)
Since the first dawn broke across this world, I have endured. Through flood and fire, through the silence between stars, I have waited. You call yourself a hero? I have consumed heroes for sport. Your courage is admirable; courage without power is simply a beautiful way to die. Come, then. Let us end this.
Know this, mortal: the darkness you fight is not your enemy, but your destiny. Every word you've typed in haste has been a gift to me, a link in the chain of your defeat. There is no victory here, only the slow realization that you never had a chance. But please, continue. I do so enjoy watching the doomed struggle.
They called me the Undying; the Eternal Dread; the Hunger Without End. Ten thousand warriors have tried to end my existence, and I remember each face with fondness. I've broken mightier champions than you. Yet here you stand, brave and foolish, with nothing but your words between you and the abyss. How very delightful.
You want to know what true power feels like? It feels like patience. I have waited a thousand years for a worthy challenger, and in all that time, none have come close. I won't lie to you - your skill impresses me. But impressing me and defeating me are two very different things. Now, let's see what you're truly made of.
