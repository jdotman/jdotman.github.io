# Boss paragraphs — Tier 2 (Level 10)
The demon lord rises from his obsidian throne and fixes his cold gaze upon you.
Long have I waited in this darkness, growing stronger with each passing year. Now you have come to challenge me? How very amusing.
Thunder crashes as the great serpent rises from the abyss below. Its scales were forged in the heart of a dying star. Do you truly believe, small mortal, that you can stand against such ancient power?
The necromancer's eyes burn with cold fire. 'I have walked this earth since before your ancestors drew their first breath,' he says. 'I have watched kingdoms fall and oceans change their course. I have seen greater heroes than you crumble at my feet. You are nothing but another soul for my collection.'
Every warrior who has come before you — and there have been many, stretching back through centuries you cannot name — believed, as you believe now, that their particular courage would be enough; not one of them was right, and the only question worth asking, as I look upon your defiant face, is whether you are any different.
