# Boss paragraphs — Tier 2 (Level 10)
The demon lord rises from his obsidian throne. 'You dare enter my domain, mortal?' he sneers. His laughter echoes off the stone walls, but you hold your ground and prepare to fight.
Long have I waited in darkness, growing stronger with each passing year. Now you've come to challenge me? How amusing. I have devoured armies, hero. What hope do your words hold against my power?
Thunder crashes as the great serpent rises from the abyss. Its scales are forged in the heart of a dying star. Do you truly believe you can stand against such power? Bold, but foolish.
The necromancer's eyes burn with cold fire. 'I've walked this earth since before your ancestors drew their first breath,' he says. 'You are nothing but another soul for my collection.'
